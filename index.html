What is Rent?

Solana accounts need space on the blockchain. To get this space, you pay a "rent deposit." If you've traded many coins, you likely have dozens of empty accounts still holding your money. Sol Claimer closes these accounts and sends the money back to you.

Is it safe?

We use secure node technology to verify your account status. Our tool is designed for volume traders who need to clean their wallets quickly and recover trapped capital without technical stress.
